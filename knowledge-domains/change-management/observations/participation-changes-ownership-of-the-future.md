# Participation changes ownership of the future

## Observation

People engage differently with a future state when they have helped shape the constraints, language, trade-offs, or practical path toward it.

## Interpretation

Participation is not consensus theater. Its value is partly diagnostic and partly relational: it improves the design while helping people see themselves in the future being built.

## Evidence Needed

- Examples where involvement changed the implementation path.
- Examples where communication alone failed to create commitment.
- Counterexamples where participation slowed needed action without improving quality.
