# Future State Alignment

## Purpose

A facilitation model for clarifying what people hope will be different before choosing solutions.

## Use When

- The situation is complex enough to require structure.
- The team needs shared language.
- Decisions need to be grounded in observed reality.

## Basic Flow

1. Clarify current reality.
2. Identify the desired future state.
3. Surface constraints and trade-offs.
4. Choose the smallest useful next action.
5. Review outcomes and update understanding.
