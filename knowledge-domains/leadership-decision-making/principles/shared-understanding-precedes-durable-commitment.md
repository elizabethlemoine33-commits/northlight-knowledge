# Shared understanding precedes durable commitment

## Statement

People can support a decision more effectively when they understand the reality, trade-offs, and reasoning that shaped it.

## Why It Matters

This principle guides practical decisions in the leadership and decision making domain. It should be tested against observations, evidence, and future client work before being treated as mature.
