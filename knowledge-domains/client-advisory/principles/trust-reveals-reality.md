# Trust reveals reality

## Statement

People disclose workarounds, concerns, and context when they feel understood and safe enough to be honest.

## Why It Matters

This principle guides practical decisions in the client advisory domain. It should be tested against observations, evidence, and future client work before being treated as mature.
