# Resistance is evidence, not enemy

## Statement

Resistance may point to unclear value, hidden cost, overlooked constraints, or legitimate flaws.

## Why It Matters

This principle guides practical decisions in the change management domain. It should be tested against observations, evidence, and future client work before being treated as mature.
