# Avoided disagreement reappears as execution risk

## Observation

Concerns that are not surfaced during decision-making often return during implementation.

## Interpretation

This observation should not be treated as a conclusion by itself. It becomes more useful when linked to repeated evidence and compared with counterexamples.
