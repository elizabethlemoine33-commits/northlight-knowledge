# Aspirational goals can hide missing plans

## Observation

Statements of desire do not create direction until translated into evidence, constraints, and action.

## Interpretation

This observation should not be treated as a conclusion by itself. It becomes more useful when linked to repeated evidence and compared with counterexamples.
