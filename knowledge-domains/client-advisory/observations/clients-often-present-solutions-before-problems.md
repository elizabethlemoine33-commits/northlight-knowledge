# Clients often present solutions before problems

## Observation

Requests such as AI, dashboards, or new software may be proposed solutions to an unidentified need.

## Interpretation

This observation should not be treated as a conclusion by itself. It becomes more useful when linked to repeated evidence and compared with counterexamples.
