# Core Terms

## Current reality

The actual conditions, constraints, capabilities, and behaviours present today.

## Destination

The future state the organisation is intentionally moving toward.

## Strategic coherence

The degree to which initiatives, resources, decisions, and outcomes meaningfully connect.
