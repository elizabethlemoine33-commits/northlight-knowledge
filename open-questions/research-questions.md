# Research Questions

> *Questions that have not yet been answered with sufficient confidence.*

---

## Purpose

Not every important question has an immediate answer.

This document records significant questions that have emerged through practice but require further observation, experimentation, or evidence before conclusions are reached.

Questions are valuable assets.

They direct future research, shape client engagements, and reveal gaps in our current understanding.

---

# Active Questions

## Organisations

- What conditions consistently distinguish organisations that sustain change from those that revert to previous behaviours?
- At what point does organisational complexity begin reducing effectiveness?
- Which indicators best predict operational resilience?

---

## Leadership

- What leadership behaviours most consistently improve organisational clarity?
- How does decision ownership evolve as organisations grow?
- Which leadership practices reduce organisational dependency?

---

## Artificial Intelligence

- Which organisational characteristics best predict successful AI adoption?
- What governance models scale effectively for small and medium-sized organisations?
- Where does AI create measurable value beyond productivity improvements?

---

## Knowledge Management

- How should organisational knowledge be structured for both human and AI consumption?
- What makes knowledge repositories remain useful over many years?
- Which forms of institutional knowledge are most frequently lost?

---

## Advisory

- Which advisory interventions consistently create lasting organisational capability?
- How can advisory success be measured after an engagement concludes?
- Which recommendations most often fail during implementation, and why?
