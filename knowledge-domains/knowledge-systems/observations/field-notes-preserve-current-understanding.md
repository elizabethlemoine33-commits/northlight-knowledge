# Field notes preserve current understanding

## Observation

Field notes capture the state of understanding at a moment in time, including uncertainty, partial interpretations, and questions that may disappear from polished outputs.

## Interpretation

The value of field notes is not only the final conclusion. They preserve the path by which an idea developed, which helps future readers assess confidence and revise the idea honestly.

## Evidence Needed

- Examples where earlier field notes clarified why a later decision was made.
- Examples where uncertainty recorded early prevented overconfidence later.
- Counterexamples where field notes created confusion because they were not reviewed or linked.
