# Projects compete when strategy is unclear

## Observation

Without clear priorities, teams often defend local initiatives rather than shared outcomes.

## Interpretation

This observation should not be treated as a conclusion by itself. It becomes more useful when linked to repeated evidence and compared with counterexamples.
