# Core Terms

## Guardrail

A boundary that enables safe action without prescribing every step.

## Control

A mechanism intended to reduce risk or improve consistency.

## Shadow system

An unofficial process, tool, spreadsheet, or workflow used because the formal system does not meet the need.
