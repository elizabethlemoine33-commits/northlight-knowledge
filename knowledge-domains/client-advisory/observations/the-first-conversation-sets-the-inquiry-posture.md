# The first conversation sets the inquiry posture

## Observation

Beginning with curiosity opens a different conversation than beginning with assessment.

## Interpretation

This observation should not be treated as a conclusion by itself. It becomes more useful when linked to repeated evidence and compared with counterexamples.
