# Models serve reality

## Observation

AI models, frameworks, and tools become more useful when they are evaluated against the real work they must support rather than against their abstract capability.

## Interpretation

This observation cautions against treating model performance as separate from workflow fit, governance, user trust, data quality, and decision responsibility.

## Evidence Needed

- Examples where a technically capable AI tool failed because it did not fit the work.
- Examples where a simpler model or workflow produced better practical outcomes.
- Counterexamples where capability alone created immediate value.
