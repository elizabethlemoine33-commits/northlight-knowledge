# Unclear decision rights slow execution

## Observation

When teams do not know who owns a decision, work often pauses, escalates, or loops through unnecessary meetings.

## Interpretation

This observation should not be treated as a conclusion by itself. It becomes more useful when linked to repeated evidence and compared with counterexamples.
