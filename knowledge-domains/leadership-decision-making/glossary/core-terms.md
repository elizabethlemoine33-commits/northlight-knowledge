# Core Terms

## Decision ownership

Clear accountability for making, communicating, and revisiting a decision.

## Shared understanding

A common enough view of reality, destination, constraints, and reasoning to support coordinated action.

## Decision latency

The time lost between recognizing that a decision is needed and making it clearly enough for action.
