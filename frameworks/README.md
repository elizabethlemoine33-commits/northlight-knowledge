# Frameworks

> *Practical applications of accumulated knowledge.*

---

## Purpose

Frameworks translate knowledge into action.

They exist to help people make decisions, solve problems, and improve organisations.

A framework should always be traceable back through:

Framework

↓

Core Principle

↓

Current Understanding

↓

Evidence

↓

Observation

Every framework should have a clear foundation.

---

## Characteristics

Good frameworks are:

- practical
- repeatable
- adaptable
- evidence-informed
- easy to understand
- straightforward to apply

Frameworks are tools.

They are not universal truths.

---
